# NextChord User Guide

## Song Viewer

The Song Viewer displays your song with lyrics and chords in a clean, distraction-free interface optimized for live performance.

### Navigation

- **Menu Button** (top-left): Opens the sidebar to navigate to other songs or setlists
- **Edit Button** (top-right): Opens the song editor to modify the song
- **Tap anywhere**: Toggle the title bar visibility for a completely clean view

### Adjusting Font Size

You can adjust the font size of lyrics and chords using multiple methods:

#### Touchscreen / Trackpad
- **Pinch-to-zoom**: Use two fingers to pinch in (decrease size) or spread out (increase size)
- Works on tablets, phones, and laptops with touchscreens or precision trackpads

#### Mouse / Keyboard
- **Ctrl + Scroll Wheel**: Hold the Ctrl key and scroll up (decrease) or down (increase)
- Standard zoom gesture used in most applications (browsers, IDEs, etc.)

#### Font Size Range
- Minimum: 12pt
- Maximum: 48pt
- Font size adjustments are smooth and responsive

### Song Display

- **Title Bar**: Shows the song title at the top (can be hidden by tapping)
- **Scrollable Content**: Scroll through the song lyrics and chords
- **ChordPro Format**: Songs are displayed with proper formatting for sections, chords, and lyrics

### Tips

- Hide the title bar during performance for maximum screen space
- Use pinch-to-zoom or Ctrl+Scroll to find your ideal font size for your performance setup
- The font size persists during your session but resets when you open a new song
